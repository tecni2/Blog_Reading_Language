My First Post

Hello, my name is Juan and I have a very good friend.

My parents are sure that black is an elegant color.

Outside, the clock shows zero hours and my body feels tired.

My head is full of thoughts about the coming month.

The building where I work has a big desk and a lot of money invested in clothes.

The animals I see in the park remind me that being in nature is important.

I like to eat delicious dishes and enjoy the pleasant weather.

Listening to music at the school in the city is my favorite hobby.

My country is beautiful and I love walking through its streets.

My car is red and I always find a place to park before the day ends.
